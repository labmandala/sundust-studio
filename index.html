Sundust ~ Dust particles in Sunlight.

Unique handmade designs incorporating gemstone, fossil, vintage, upcycled, and various natural components.

Philosophy

S undust as an extension of my creative work embodies my ideas about about worldly relations and attachments, recycling of elements and energies, the creative process, fertility, death and rebirth, cosmos, and the transient nature of life. Inspired by poet Rumi: “I am Dust Particles in Sunlight.”
